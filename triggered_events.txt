hunger > 10 % > 70
	text The animal is hungry!
	option Feed it
		test food >= 10
		effects hunger - 5 food - 10
	option Don't feed it

hunger >= 20
	text The animal is starving!
	option Feed it
		test food >= 10
		effects hunger = 15 food - 10
	option Don't feed it
		effects lose

onTile Forest
	text You are in a desert!
	option Yes
		effects event agree
	option No
